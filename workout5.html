Core Strength

Focus on building a strong and stable core with this specialized workout program.

This program targets all the muscles in your core, including abs, obliques, and lower back, to improve stability and strength.

  • Day 1: Abdominal Circuit - Crunches, Leg Raises
  • Day 2: Core Stability - Planks, Side Planks
  • Day 3: Lower Back Strength - Superman, Deadlifts
  • Day 4: Active Recovery - Light Cardio & Stretching
  • Day 5: Oblique Focus - Russian Twists, Side Bends
  • Day 6: Full-Body Core Circuit
  • Day 7: Rest Day
