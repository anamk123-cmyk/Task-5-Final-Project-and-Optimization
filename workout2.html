Build Your Strength

Focus on muscle building with our specially designed strength training program.

This program is ideal for those looking to increase muscle mass and strength. It includes compound lifts and isolation exercises.

  • Day 1: Compound Lifts - Squats, Deadlifts
  • Day 2: Upper Body Strength - Bench Press, Rows
  • Day 3: Lower Body Strength - Lunges, Leg Press
  • Day 4: Core and Stability - Planks, Russian Twists
  • Day 5: Full-Body Strength - Mixed Circuit
  • Day 6: Active Recovery - Light Cardio & Stretching
  • Day 7: Rest Day
